Installation Guide

1

Download the Installer

Click the appropriate download button for your operating system above. The download will start automatically.

2

Run the Installer

Windows: Double-click the .exe file and follow the setup wizard.

macOS: Open the .dmg file and drag Cargolyze to your Applications folder.

Linux: Make the .AppImage file executable and run it:

chmod +x Cargolyze.AppImage && ./Cargolyze.AppImage
3

Activate Your License

Launch Cargolyze and sign in with your account credentials. Your subscription will be automatically activated.

4

Start Tracking

Import your shipment data or connect to your logistics platform to begin automated tracking.
